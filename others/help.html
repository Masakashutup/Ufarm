Help Center


Welcome to the Ufarm Help Center. Here, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions, as well as resources to help you get the most out of our platform.

If you're a farmer, our help center provides guidance on how to list your products, manage your orders, and connect with buyers. We also offer tips and best practices for sustainable farming practices and how to promote your products effectively.

If you're a buyer, our help center provides information on how to search for products, place orders, and track deliveries. We also offer resources to help you learn more about the farmers and the products you're purchasing, so you can make informed decisions about the food you eat.
