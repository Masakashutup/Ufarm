About us

Welcome to Ufarm, the online platform connecting Ugandan farmers with buyers across the country. We believe that every farmer deserves fair access to markets, and every buyer should have access to fresh, locally grown produce.

Our platform makes it easy for farmers to showcase their products and connect with buyers. We work directly with farmers to ensure that their produce is of the highest quality and meets our strict standards for freshness and sustainability.

Our Mission
At Ufarm, we are committed to building a better future for Ugandan agriculture. By supporting small-scale farmers and promoting sustainable practices, we are helping to create a more resilient and equitable food system. Thank you for choosing to support Ugandan farmers and for joining us in our mission to create a more sustainable and just world.
